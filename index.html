AmVish_1.0

A robot that used ChatGPT's voice mode (v3) to interact and also had a heart which gave the robot its life. It was one of the first thing we had built with an Arduino Board.

February 2025

AI Bin - a dustbin capable of sorting the waste even before it goes inside

We used Audio Classification to make the bin idendify the sort of waste going into it and automatically put them in different sections of the bin, all with zero human inference.

December 2025

AmViSH_2.0

A little attempt to give an LLM a body, this robot identified itself and was capable of using all the actuators and sensors on its body with its own intent.
It was the first time we had a robot that could do things on its own without any human inference, and it was a huge milestone for us.
